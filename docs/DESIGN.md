# Game Design Document

## Story
The player is the Swallower who merges powers of creation and destruction to save/integrate the 6 realms before they are destroyed.

## Mechanics
- Swallowing: Absorb environments, entities with their behaviors.
- Personal Realm: Private building space with full simulation.
- Cross-realm interactions, interspecies marriage, etc.
- Procedural worlds with unique biomes per realm.

## Systems
- RPG Character Stats
- Building & Crafting
- NPC Management & Jobs
- Deep History Simulation
